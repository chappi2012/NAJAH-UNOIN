## ⚡ **Deploy in 3 Steps:**

### **Step 1: Push to GitHub**

If you haven't already, upload your files to GitHub:

1. Go to [github.com/new](https://github.com/new)
2. **Repository name:** `najah-union`
3. Click **"Create repository"**
4. Click **"Add file" → "Upload files"**
5. Upload these files:
   - `index.html` (the dark colorful version)
   - `README.md`
   - `.gitignore`
6. Click **"Commit changes"**

---

### **Step 2: Connect to Vercel**

1. Go to [vercel.com](https://vercel.com)
2. Click **"Sign Up"** → Select **"GitHub"**
3. **Authorize** Vercel to access your GitHub
4. Click **"Import Project"**
5. **Select:** `najah-union` repository
6. Click **"Import"**

---

### **Step 3: Deploy!**

That's literally it! ✨

Vercel will:
- ✅ Automatically deploy your site
- ✅ Give you a live URL in 30 seconds
- ✅ Auto-update whenever you push to GitHub
- ✅ Provide FREE SSL certificate (HTTPS)
- ✅ Give you a custom domain (FREE)

## 🔄 **Update Your Website:**

1. Edit `index.html` on your computer
2. Commit and push to GitHub:
   ```bash
   git add .
   git commit -m "Updated teams"
   git push
   ```
3. **Vercel auto-deploys** (1-2 minutes)
4. Your live website updates automatically! 🎉

---

## 🌐 **Add a Custom Domain (Optional)**

1. Go to your Vercel dashboard
2. Click your project
3. Go to **"Settings" → "Domains"**
4. Click **"Add Domain"**
5. Enter your domain (e.g., `najah-union.com`)
6. Follow DNS setup (usually 5 minutes)

Now your site is at `najah-union.com` instead of `vercel.app`!
